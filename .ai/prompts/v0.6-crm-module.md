# Objective

Implement the CRM module.

The CRM manages professional relationships for a freelance game audio business.

The module should be completely independent of the Notion SDK.

Use the existing Schema Engine.

---

# Business Context

Unlike traditional CRMs, this database is relationship-driven rather than sales-driven.

It should help manage long-term relationships with people who may become collaborators, clients, employers or referral sources.

Typical contacts include:

- Indie developers
- Game studios
- Audio directors
- Producers
- Sound designers
- Composers
- Recruiters
- Publishers
- Outsourcing studios
- Middleware partners
- Event organizers

The CRM should support years of relationship building.
