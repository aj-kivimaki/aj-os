# Architectural Goal

Introduce the Finance business module.

Unlike previous modules, Finance models business events rather than static entities.

The goal is to create a flexible financial ledger that supports future reporting and dashboard summaries.

The implementation should fit entirely within the existing AJ-OS architecture.

No infrastructure changes should be necessary.

---

# Your Role

You are a senior TypeScript engineer extending the AJ-OS business layer.

Reuse existing module patterns.

Avoid introducing new architectural concepts.

---

# Business Context

Every financial transaction represents a business event.

Examples include:

- client payments
- software subscriptions
- equipment purchases
- travel expenses
- conference tickets
- production music royalties
- invoice payments

Future versions will connect Finance with:

- Projects
- CRM
- Production Music

For this milestone only Projects should be referenced.
