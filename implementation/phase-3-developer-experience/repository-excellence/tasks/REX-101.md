# Purpose

**Why does this task exist?**

Because a **mandatory AJS-007 deliverable was skipped five consecutive times and nobody noticed.**

All five SPEC-003 milestones were frozen without a Retrospective (F-018). That violates §4.7 stage
7, §8.1 (a Lifecycle Deliverable — *"Authored by the author; accepted by the reviewer"*),
`SPEC-FREEZE-REVIEW.md` Step 8, and its own Freeze Decision box: *"[ ] Retrospective completed
(accumulated, not overwritten)"*. SPEC-002 produced four.

The consequence is not bookkeeping. **AJS-007 §9.2 designated SPEC-003 as the evidence that would
validate or revise the standard**, and §3 makes the retrospective the *only* channel through which
that evidence can travel upward. The evidence exists — five milestones, 26 tasks, 11 decisions,
two Frozen Plan Change Proposals, five plan amendments — and it has never reached the standard.
AJS-007 is still Draft, still resting on SPEC-002 alone, **because this document was never
written.**

---

# Objective

Produce `retrospectives/RETROSPECTIVE.md` in the SPEC-003 implementation package, covering M1–M5,
reconstructed from the surviving engineering record, and **explicitly labelled as reconstructed.**

  provenance**.
- Any change to SPEC-003's frozen implementation package (README/MILESTONES/decisions/tasks).
- Re-litigating frozen decisions.
- REX's own retrospective (that lands at the M1 Freeze Review).

# Implementation Notes

**Label it reconstructed, prominently, in the header.** A retrospective that silently implies it was
written contemporaneously is worse than none: it launders reconstruction as observation, and every
future reader weighs it wrongly. Say what was recovered, from what, and **what is gone** —
contemporaneous surprise, the alternatives considered and discarded in the moment, the reasoning
that never reached a document.

**Follow SPEC-002's retrospective shape** (What worked well · What surprised us · Engineering
discoveries · Process improvements · Deferred improvements) per `SPEC-FREEZE-REVIEW.md` Step 8. Do
not invent a new format for a document whose absence is the finding.

**The §9.2 question is the one that matters.** SPEC-003's shape genuinely differs from SPEC-002's —
it carries a non-deterministic seam (model extraction), real filesystem persistence, and a live CLI,
where SPEC-002 was a deterministic in-memory pipeline. §9.2 says such a specification *"carries
particular evidentiary weight, because it tests whether the practice generalises."* **That is the
question this document exists to answer.** Answer it directly.

**Do not manufacture lessons.** Where the record does not support a claim, say the record does not
support it. An honest gap is evidence; an invented insight is noise that will be cited later as
though it were observed.

**Candidate material the record does support** (verify each before asserting):
- **The FPCP mechanism worked.** Four frozen-plan gaps surfaced during M5; each was raised rather
  than absorbed, scoped narrowly, and approved before dependent work began (EOS-D10, EOS-D11).
  This is direct evidence for §7.2.
- **Documentation Synchronization (§7.4) failed silently** — a *Mandatory* mechanism. MILESTONES.md
  v1.32 claims *"Documentation synchronized per AJS-007 §7.4"* while `src/end-of-session/README.md`
  still says "No behavior exists yet" (F-005). **The mechanism has no verification step**, which is
  a finding about the standard, not about the author.
- **The retrospective's own absence** is the strongest evidence in the document: a Mandatory
  deliverable was skipped five times across five reviewer-declared freezes, and **the Freeze
  Decision checklist that lists it did not catch it.** Ask what would have.
- **Five plan amendments in M5.** MILESTONES.md v1.32 already flags this: *"That the plan needed
  five amendments is itself worth reflecting on at the Retrospective."* The reflection is owed.
- **The typecheck gotcha hid three real defects** — evidence about validation, and now REX-M2's
  F-027.

**Recommendations travel upward as proposals, never as changes.** Phrase each as a recommendation
with its evidence, and stop there. Whether AJS-007 changes is the reviewer's call at the AJS layer
under §10.2 — not this task's, and not this package's.
