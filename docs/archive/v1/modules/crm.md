# CRM Module

## Purpose

The CRM module manages professional relationships.

Unlike traditional sales-focused CRMs, the AJ-OS CRM is designed for freelancers working in the game industry.

Its primary goal is to help maintain meaningful long-term relationships rather than manage sales pipelines.

---

# Responsibilities

The CRM module is responsible for:

- Tracking professional contacts
- Recording communication history
- Planning follow-ups
- Managing relationship status
- Organizing networking opportunities
- Supporting long-term collaboration

---

# Core Properties

The module currently includes:

- Name
- Company
- Role
- Relationship Status
- Contact Source
- Last Contact
- Next Follow-up
- Interests
- Notes

Additional properties may be added as business requirements evolve.

---

# Relations

Future relationships may include:

---

# Templates

The CRM module provides templates for common contact types.

Examples include:

- Game Studio
- Indie Developer
- Audio Director
- Recruiter
- Publisher

Templates provide sensible defaults while remaining fully customizable.

---

# Dashboard Integration

The CEO Dashboard summarizes CRM activity.

Typical information includes:

- Follow-ups due
- Waiting replies
- New contacts
- Highest-priority relationship

The dashboard links directly to the CRM database for detailed management.

---

# Design Principles

The CRM focuses on relationships rather than transactions.

It is intended to support:

- Networking
- Collaboration
- Career development
- Long-term professional connections

The module remains independent from infrastructure and synchronization.

---

# Future Ideas

Potential future enhancements include:

- Communication history
- Meeting notes
- Contact reminders
- Social links
- Opportunity tracking
- Collaboration history

These capabilities can be introduced without changing the underlying architecture.

---

# Guiding Principle

The CRM answers one question:

> **Who should I build relationships with next?**

Strong professional relationships are one of the most valuable long-term assets for a freelance business.
