# Production Music Module

## Purpose

The Production Music module manages commercially licensable music.

It supports the creation, organization and publication of production music intended for music libraries, sync licensing and long-term passive income.

Unlike project-specific compositions, production music is treated as a reusable business asset.

---

# Responsibilities

The Production Music module is responsible for:

- Organizing music cues
- Tracking production status
- Managing catalogue growth
- Recording submission progress
- Tracking publication status
- Preparing music for library submission

The module provides a structured view of the entire production music catalogue.

---

# Core Properties

The module currently includes:

- Title
- Status
- Genre
- Mood
- Duration
- BPM
- Key
- Library
- Published
- Notes

Additional metadata may be introduced as publishing workflows evolve.

# Templates

The module provides templates for common cue types.

Examples include:

- Cinematic Cue
- Hybrid Action
- Ambient
- Horror
- Production Music Album Track

Templates provide consistent starting points for new compositions.

---

# Dashboard Integration

The CEO Dashboard summarizes catalogue progress.

Typical information includes:

- Draft cues
- Ready cues
- Submitted cues
- Published cues

The Dashboard recommends preparing library submissions whenever enough finished material is available.

The Dashboard links directly to the Production Music database for detailed management.

---

# Design Principles

Production Music treats every cue as a long-term business asset.

The objective is not only to finish music, but to build a catalogue capable of generating recurring income over time.

The module remains independent from storage, synchronization and presentation.

---

# Guiding Principle
