AJ-OS Principles

1. Everything is generated by code.

2. No manual edits inside Notion.

3. Projects are the center of the business.

4. Every piece of information should exist only once.

5. Relations over duplication.

6. Automate repetitive work.

7. Design for five years, not five weeks.

8. Documentation drives implementation.

9. Small modules over large files.

10. The system should help make decisions, not just store information.
