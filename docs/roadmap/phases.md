# AJ-OS Development Phases

## Vision

AJ-OS is developed in clearly defined phases.

Each phase has a distinct purpose.

The project evolves from a workspace generator into a complete business operating system and, eventually, an intelligent business platform.

Architecture should stabilize early.

Business capabilities should continue evolving indefinitely.

---

# Phase 1

## Business Operating System

Status

Current

Goal

Create a complete business operating system capable of managing a freelance game audio business.

Focus

- Business Modules
- Schema Engine
- Module Registry
- Translation Layer
- Workspace Synchronization
- CEO Dashboard

Milestones

- Foundation
- Projects
- CRM
- Portfolio
- Production Music
- Game Jams
- Finance
- Relations
- CEO Dashboard

Deliverable

A complete, connected Notion workspace generated entirely from code.

Result

Users can manage their business through AJ-OS.

---

# Phase 2

## Business Intelligence

Goal

Transform business data into actionable insights.

Focus

- Business Rules
- Morning Brief
- Weekly Report
- Recommendations
- Goal Tracking
- Trend Analysis

Example Questions

What should I focus on today?

Which client needs attention?

Is my production music catalogue growing?

How healthy is the business?

Deliverable

The Dashboard becomes an executive decision support system rather than a navigation page.

---

# Phase 3

## Automation

Goal

Reduce repetitive work.

Focus

- Scheduled synchronization
- Recurring reminders
- CRM follow-up automation
- Production music submission reminders
- Portfolio maintenance reminders
- Invoice reminders

Deliverable

AJ-OS actively supports business operations instead of simply reporting them.

---

# Phase 4

## Analytics

Goal

Measure long-term business performance.

Focus

- Revenue analytics
- Project analytics
- Portfolio growth
- Production music statistics
- Client activity
- Goal completion
- Annual reports

Deliverable

Historical insight into business performance.

---

# Phase 5

## Intelligence

Goal

Provide strategic guidance.

Focus

- AI-assisted recommendations
- Forecasting
- Opportunity detection
- Risk detection
- Natural language reporting
- Smart planning

Examples

You have enough finished tracks to prepare another album.

This client has not been contacted for three months.

Revenue is growing slower than expected.

Deliverable

AJ-OS evolves from an operating system into an intelligent business assistant.

---

# Guiding Principles

Every phase builds on the previous one.

Architecture should remain stable.

Business capabilities should grow incrementally.

Automation should never replace user control.

Artificial intelligence should assist decision-making rather than replace it.
